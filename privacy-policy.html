Privacy Policy (गोपनीयता नीति)

Aapki privacy humare liye sabse zaruri hai. Yahan padhein ki hum aapka data kaise manage karte hain.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Hum Kaunsi Jankari Ikhatthi Karte Hain?

Jab aap Digital Hub Hindi par aate hain, toh hum aapse koi personal jankari (jaise password ya bank details) nahi maangte. Agar aap hume contact form ke zariye message bhejte hain, tabhi hume aapka naam aur email address milta hai, jise hum puri tarah surakshit rakhte hain.

2. Cookies (कुकीज़) Ka Upyog

Humari website aapke browsing experience ko behtar banane ke liye 'Cookies' ka use karti hai. Iske alawa, Google AdSense jaise third-party vendors bhi aapko aapki pasand ke ads dikhane ke liye cookies ka use kar sakte hain.

3. Third-Party Links

Humari website par dusri (third-party) websites ke links hain (jaise AI tools aur earning websites). Jab aap un links par click karke dusri website par jaate hain, toh unki apni Privacy Policy lagu hoti hai. Hum unki websites ke content ya policy ke liye zimmewar nahi hain.

4. Bachon Ki Privacy (Children's Privacy)

Digital Hub Hindi 13 saal se kam umar ke bachon ke liye nahi banayi gayi hai, aur hum jan-bujhkar unka koi bhi data collect nahi karte.

5. Humse Sampark Karein

Agar aapko humari is Privacy Policy se juda koi bhi sawal puchna hai, toh aap kisi bhi waqt humse Contact Page ke zariye sampark kar sakte hain.
